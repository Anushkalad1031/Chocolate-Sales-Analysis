# Chocolate-Sales-Analysis
Excel-based analysis of chocolate sales showing product performance, top sellers, sales trends, contributions by product, and sales by country. Includes pivot tables, charts, and interactive slicers for easy insights and decision-making.


## Chocolate Sales Analysis overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing chocolate product sales. The dashboard provides insights into top products, sales trends, product contributions, and country-wise sales.

## Key Features
1. Sales by Product – Bar chart showing total sales per product.

2. Top Five Best Sellers – Highlights highest-selling products.

3. Sales Trend – Line chart tracking monthly sales.

4. Contribution by Product – Percentage of total sales per product.

5. Sales by Country – Pie chart showing geographical sales distribution.

6. Interactive Slicers – Filter by Product, Date, Boxes Shipped, and Country.


## Purpose
Helps monitor sales performance, identify top products, analyze trends, and support data-driven decisions.


##Tools
Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers)
